Event Overview

Let’s make this event unforgettable—where strategy meets excitement and every move brings us closer to victory!

Round 1 : Quiz

Engage in a fun and interactive quiz covering chess history and technological advancements. Points are awarded based on accuracy and speed. The top scorers will qualify for the next round.

Round 2: Play Against AI

Challenge yourself by competing against an intermediate-level chess engine. To advance, you must checkmate the AI. Only successful players will move forward to the next round.

Round 3: Math Puzzle

Solve quick logic-based math puzzles to earn the right to choose your chess piece color. Accuracy and speed will be key in this round. The top 8 players will secure their spot in the final stage.

Finals

Round 4: On-Board Finals

Players will compete in a Swiss System format with a 15-minute timer and a 5-second increment per move. Matches will be conducted under FIDE rules to ensure fair play. The final winners will be determined based on their performance in this round.
